About

Who we are

Established in 2015, Joe Rooter INC Sewer and Drain Cleaning has built a reputation for excellence and reliability in sewer and drain cleaning services. Based in Queens, NY, we are committed to providing top-tier solutions for both residential and commercial clients. Our goal is to be the trusted partner you turn to for maintaining clean and efficient plumbing systems.

  • Expert services tailored to meet your specific needs.
  • Committed to prompt and reliable service every time.
  • Focused on maintaining high standards of professionalism.

Unlike many sewer cleaning companies in Queens, NY and the region that offer limited assistance, we offer a complete service package. We can keep main and secondary sewer lines free from blockages to ensure property water flow and prevent backflow. We can also clean clogged drains and handle plumbing jobs of varying complexity. Your satisfaction is our main priority.
